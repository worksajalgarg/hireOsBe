# Design partners — Week 1 exit-gate dependency

The PRD's 12-week roadmap (Section 11, Week 1 exit gate) requires: *"Founder sign-off; three real role packs and anonymised resume set available."* Section 11 "Critical dependencies" also states: *"Two design partners willing to provide real roles, anonymised resumes and weekly recruiter/hiring-manager feedback"* is a blocking dependency for the whole 12-week plan, not just Week 1.

**This is owned by the founder/business side, not engineering.** It is tracked here so it is not silently dropped while engineering scaffolding proceeds in parallel.

## What's needed
- [ ] Two enterprise design partners (or one paying enterprise customer) confirmed, per Appendix B's pilot commercial structure (PRD).
- [ ] Three real role packs (job descriptions + hiring-manager context) from those partners.
- [ ] An anonymised resume set for those roles, usable for engineering benchmarks without exposing real candidate PII.
- [ ] A named executive sponsor per partner and a commitment to weekly recruiter/hiring-manager feedback sessions (PRD Section 12, "Operating cadence").

## Status
Not yet started — flagged as a Week 1 blocking item per the roadmap's own exit criteria.
